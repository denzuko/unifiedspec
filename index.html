Heritage
Solarized
Tonal palette. CIELAB-calibrated base tones for light and dark modes.
Okabe-Ito
Semantic color. CVD-safe accent assignments across all color vision types.
Plan 9 / Acme
Structure. Utility-first composition, tag-bar grammar, worm strip, user-initiates focus.
Solaris / CDE
Chrome. CDE titlebar, Sun orange logo block, Motif 3D buttons.
